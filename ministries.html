Our Ministries

Discover ways to serve, grow, and connect with our church family

Get Involved

At St Johns Chapel, we believe everyone has a place to serve and grow. Our ministries provide opportunities for all ages to connect with God and each other.

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry is dedicated to nurturing the faith of our youngest members. We provide a safe, loving environment where children can learn about God's love through Bible stories, songs, crafts, and games.

We believe that children are a gift from God, and we take seriously the responsibility to teach them the ways of the Lord. Our dedicated team of volunteers ensures that every child feels welcomed and valued.

Meeting Time: Sundays at 9:00 AM during the main service

Age Groups: 0-3 years (Nursery), 4-6 years (Beginners), 7-12 years (Juniors)

Get Involved

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is designed to help teenagers and young adults navigate life's challenges while growing in their relationship with Christ. We provide a space where young people can ask questions, build friendships, and develop a faith that lasts.

Through Bible studies, discussions, social events, and service projects, we equip our youth to be leaders in their schools, communities, and the church. We believe in the potential of every young person to make a difference for God's kingdom.

Meeting Time: Saturdays at 3:00 PM

Age Group: 13-25 years

Join Us

Women's Fellowship

The Women's Fellowship brings together women of all ages for spiritual growth, mutual support, and service. We meet regularly to study God's Word, pray for one another, and share life's joys and challenges.

Our ministry provides opportunities for women to use their gifts and talents to serve the church and community. From Bible studies to outreach projects, we are committed to helping women grow in their faith and make a positive impact.

Meeting Time: First Sunday of the month at 3:00 PM

Activities: Bible Study, Prayer Meetings, Community Outreach, Mentorship

Learn More

Men's Fellowship

The Men's Fellowship is committed to helping men grow in their faith and fulfill their God-given roles as husbands, fathers, and leaders. We provide a supportive environment where men can be honest about their struggles and encourage one another.

Through regular meetings, breakfast gatherings, and service projects, we build strong bonds of brotherhood and accountability. We challenge men to be godly examples in their homes, workplaces, and communities.

Meeting Time: Second Saturday of the month at 7:00 AM (Breakfast Meeting)

Activities: Breakfast Meetings, Bible Study, Service Projects, Mentorship

Join Us

Choir Ministry

Our Choir Ministry leads the congregation in worship through music. We believe that music is a powerful way to praise God and prepare hearts to receive His Word. Our choir includes members of all ages who have a passion for worship.

We practice regularly to prepare for Sunday services and special events. The choir also participates in community events and annual choir festivals. If you have a gift for singing or playing instruments, we invite you to join us.

Practice Time: Saturdays at 4:00 PM

Requirements: A heart for worship and commitment to regular practice
