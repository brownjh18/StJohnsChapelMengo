About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the people who lead our church family

Our Story

St Johns Chapel, Mengo has a rich history of faith, service, and community that spans over a century. Founded in the early 1900s, our church has been a spiritual home for generations of believers in the Mengo area and beyond.

What began as a small gathering of faithful Christians has grown into a vibrant community of believers committed to worshiping God, studying His Word, and serving our neighbors. Throughout our history, we have witnessed God's faithfulness in times of joy and challenge, and we continue to trust in His guidance for our future.

Our church building, with its beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere, stands as a testament to the dedication of those who came before us. It serves as a place of refuge, worship, and celebration for our congregation and the wider community.

Today, we carry forward the legacy of those who built this church, remaining committed to the Gospel of Jesus Christ and the transformation it brings to individuals, families, and communities.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who worship passionately, love sacrificially, and serve faithfully in our community and beyond.

We are committed to sharing the Good News of Jesus Christ with everyone, nurturing believers in their spiritual growth, and equipping them for ministry and service.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered community that transforms lives through the power of the Gospel, raising generations of believers who impact society with God's love and truth.

We envision a church where every person experiences the transforming love of Christ, finds their purpose in God's kingdom, and becomes an agent of positive change in the world.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our church community

Biblical Foundation

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and the ultimate authority for faith and life. We are committed to teaching and living by its truths.

Prayerful Dependence

We recognize our need for God's guidance and power through prayer. Prayer is central to all we do as a church family.

Love & Compassion

We strive to love God wholeheartedly and love our neighbors as ourselves. We show compassion to those in need and welcome all people.

Community & Fellowship

We value authentic relationships and genuine fellowship. We support, encourage, and care for one another as members of God's family.

Service & Stewardship

We believe every believer is called to serve. We use our God-given gifts, time, and resources to serve the church and community.

Growth & Discipleship

We are committed to spiritual growth and making disciples. We encourage continuous learning and maturity in the Christian faith.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, authoritative for faith and practice.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, resurrection, and ascension to heaven.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. It is a gift from God, not earned by works.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit who indwells, guides, and empowers believers for holy living and service.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and mission in the world.
