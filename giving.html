Giving

Support the ministry with your tithes and offerings

Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and an expression of our gratitude to God for His abundant blessings. When we give, we acknowledge that everything we have comes from God, and we return a portion to Him in thanksgiving.

At St Johns Chapel, we believe in the biblical principle of tithing (giving 10% of our income) and freewill offerings. Your generous giving supports the work of the church, including:

  • Ministry operations and outreach programs
  • Support for pastors and church staff
  • Community service and charity work
  • Church maintenance and facilities
  • Mission support and evangelism

Types of Giving

Tithes

A tithe (10% of your income) is a biblical principle that supports the general work of the church, including ministry operations, staff support, and facility maintenance.

Offerings

Freewill offerings given above and beyond the tithe. These support special projects, outreach programs, and help those in need within our community.

Missions

Designated giving to support missionary work, church planting, and evangelism efforts locally and beyond.

Benevolence

Contributions to help church members and community members in financial distress, including medical needs, food, and shelter.

Building Fund

Contributions toward church building projects, renovations, and facility improvements.

Children & Youth

Support for children's ministry, youth programs, and activities that nurture the next generation of believers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my giving tax-deductible?

Yes, St Johns Chapel is a registered religious organization. Please request a receipt for your giving, which can be used for tax purposes where applicable.

Can I designate my giving to a specific ministry?

Yes, you can designate your giving to specific ministries or projects. Please indicate your preference when giving, either on your offering envelope or in the reference for mobile money transfers.

How can I get a giving statement?

You can request a giving statement from the church office. We provide annual statements to all members who request them. Contact our treasurer for more information.

Can I set up recurring giving?

Yes, you can set up standing orders with your bank for regular transfers to the church account. This helps ensure consistent support for the ministry.

Who can I contact about giving questions?

Please contact our church treasurer or the church office for any questions about giving. You can reach us at +256 414 123 456 or email finance@stjohnschapelmengo.org.
