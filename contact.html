Plan Your Visit

What to expect when you visit St Johns Chapel

Parking

Free parking is available on the church premises. Our parking attendants will guide you to available spots.

Children

Children are welcome! We have Sunday School classes for all ages during the service.

Dress Code

There's no strict dress code. Come as you are - whether in your Sunday best or casual attire.

Fellowship

After the service, join us for refreshments and fellowship in the church hall. We'd love to meet you!

Need Prayer?

We believe in the power of prayer. If you need prayer for any situation, we're here to pray with you and for you.
